Supply Chain Management

Whether you’re a producer, consumer or trader, SCM is setup to provide its customers and strategic partners innovative, cost-effective solutions to meet the demands of an ever-evolving supply chain.

Our identity is rooted in the process of efficiently and cost-effectively planning, implementing and controlling the operations of a supply chain. With industry leading carrier payment terms and a proven track record of delivering consistent freight to its carrier partners, SCM has developed one of the top carrier networks in the industry.

Local Drayage

Intermodal trucking and drayage solutions provide customers a cost-effective, environmentally sensitive transportation option. Close proximity between SCM’s warehousing operations and port facilities in Savannah, Houston and Dallas provide SCM with the unique ability to manage your cargo from its origin to the port.

Truckload Freight

SCM’s trucking business utilizes advanced technology to broker freight between a diverse customer and carrier base to consistently generate efficient, reliable service for both customers and carriers. SCM offers its customers and carriers real-time load monitoring and accounts payable/receivable access through its online platform.

Warehousing and Distribution

SCM is one of the largest 3PL providers of warehouse space in the Southeast and also maintains a significant presence in Houston, TX. SCM is on the forefront of warehousing technology, providing its customers with easy to access, internet based, inventory management solutions.
